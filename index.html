I am a PhD student in the ECE Department at the University of Washington in Seattle, where I am advised by Prof. Serena Eley and am an Accelerating Quantum-Enabled Technologies (AQET) fellow. I am broadly interested in materials and devices for developing scalable quantum systems.

Previously, I was an undergrad in the UC Berkeley EECS Department, where I worked with Prof. Alp Sipahigil on superconducting quantum devices. I have also worked at Sandia National Laboratories in Albuquerque, New Mexico on neuromorphic computing.
